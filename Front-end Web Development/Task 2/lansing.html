Lansing is the capital of Michigan.

  • Population: 112,644
  • Incorporated in: 1859
  • Region: Ingham County
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average income: $48,962

Did you know?

It took six years to build the Michigan Capitol Building, which has more than nine acres of hand-painted surfaces. Guided tours of the 140-year-old building are offered every half hour, for free. The building's architect, Elijah Myers, also designed capitol buildings in Colorado, Idaho and Texas. (MLive, 2017)
